Welcome

Welcome to Four Seasons Heating Ltd, a South Wiltshire based company specialising in the maintenance, repair and replacement of domestic heating systems. ‘Our reputation is built on a balance of reliability, professionalism and trustworthiness’.

We’re a family run company which is friendly and reliable. All aspects of heating and plumbing by qualified and registered gas engineers and plumbers. We’re dedicated to providing an affordable and efficient service for our customers.

Please don’t hesitate to contact us to discuss any enquires you may have.

Services

Gas & LPG Boiler Services

Boiler services should be done annually to keep your boiler safe and efficient. If you have boiler warranty this is normally dependant on having it serviced annually.

Heating & Plumbing Maintenance

We’re here to help with any heating and plumbing problems, whether it’s a boiler repair to get your heating and hot water back up and running or those leaking taps in the bathroom.

Boiler Installations

Professional installed new boilers with manufacture guarantee up to 10 years.

Landlord Gas Safety Certificates

Landlords legally have to ensure they have a Gas Safety Certificate renewed every 12 months. Please contact us for a competitive quote.
